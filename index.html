Introduction

“Something small and cute”
Chiikawa and friends bring you a heartwarming story filled with fun, friendship, and a few tearful moments.

Even when things get a little tough, there’s always someone kind nearby.

The beloved manga Chiikawa by illustrator Nagano is now an adorable anime!
